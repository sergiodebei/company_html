Our company culture

Do you have a sense of adventure? At Cygnific, we are inspired by the spirit of travel and energised by the dynamic airline industry. As a representative of KLM, Air France or Delta Air Lines, you will help our customers to embark on their own adventures by arranging their travel and accommodation. You'll be doing this in an open and friendly environment with great employment conditions and plenty of opportunities to develop, so that you can reach your full potential!

Your colleagues

Inspired as we are by travel at Cygnific, it's only natural that we have an international team. Your colleagues originate from all over the world, so you can broaden your horizons right here at work. We believe that operating as a team is a key factor in our success. We organise several social events during the year so you and your colleagues can get to know each other better. If you're an expat, this is a great way to kick-start your social life in the Netherlands!

At Cygnific, our customer contact teams are made up of around 20 people supported by a senior agent and an experienced mentor who is an expert in the product and the system. As a commercial company, we strive to achieve the best possible results with the greatest efficiency. You and your colleagues will receive individual on-the-job coaching from your supervisor to facilitate this, and we will monitor the development of your qualities so that we can empower you to deliver the best results.

Training

Travel is not the only way to broaden your horizons! Cygnific's training department provides you with all of the training you need to master and enhance your skills. As a customer contact representative, you will start out with an introductory training course to develop the basic skills you need. Afterwards, you can choose to take any of a number of other courses available in additional fields of expertise, or undergo training in the Lean Six Sigma management philosophy that plays a central role in the way we work.

Working times

At Cygnific, we strive to be there for our customers whenever they need us, at any time of day or night. We have a number of telephone lines that are open 24/7 and of course, KLM’s social media platform is monitored around the clock. Depending on the team or department in which you work, this means you could be working fixed office hours, or you could be on a rotation roster in which you know your working hours in advance, or you could even be on a flexible roster that you determine yourself. No matter what your schedule is, Cygnific operates with standard shifts of 8.5 hours, with two coffee breaks and a meal break.

Working in the Netherland

The Netherlands is a happy little country where things are very well organized. With as a result that living standards are high and risks are low. But it creates obligations.

Let’s start with the most essential: you may need a working permit which you have to apply for. Next: you need a tax and social security number (check out the Dutch tax office), a Dutch bank account and a Dutch health insurance. And of course, you will have to find a place to live. In the Netherlands public transport is on point, distances are small and cycling is healthy. But hey, don’t worry, whatever the challenge, we will give you all the help we can. From mediation, support and advice – our Hire Coordinator will guide you smoothly through your first months within Cygnific – to favorable conditions regarding your health insurance.

Below you will find comprehensive information about what you need to do to be able to work in the Netherlands.

How do I aqcuire a BSN/Social security number?

If you still live abroad and like to work for Cygnific it’s good to know that you need a combined number for tax and social security (Dutch abbreviation: BSN) and a Dutch bank account number. A BSN is provided by the Dutch tax office. To open a Dutch bank account you need to have a postal address in the Netherlands. Cygnific can assist you with both.
