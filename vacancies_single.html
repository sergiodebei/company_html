About Cygnific

Cygnific is one of the largest Sales & Service Centres for Air France KLM and is a full subsidiary of KLM. We take care of their customer contacts by phone, mail, chat and social media. We serve 22 European countries as well as the Dutch Antilles, Surinam and Israel in 14 languages; if passengers or travel agents contact KLM, Air France or Delta Air Lines we assist them.


Cygnific employs over 800 people from all over Europe and the world. Cygnific is headquartered in Amsterdam, the Netherlands with offices in both Amsterdam and Enschede. We also are in charge of the operational steering of the Sales & Service Centres in Manila, London (Wembley), Delhi and outsourced activities in Tirana.

About B2C

Our B2C department with 160 colleagues in Amsterdam serves direct customers, covering all segments from top tier to inexperienced/infrequent travellers, going on a trip for leisure or business. Whether the customer needs information about the booking she/he made, wants to check in by telephone or wants to make or change a (new) booking, at B2C he will find the right help. We serve customers of KLM, Air France and Delta in the following languages: English, Dutch, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, French, Danish, Swedish, Norwegian and Finnish. Our B2C department in The Netherlands is open 24/7 to support the customers.


Besides the B2C department in Amsterdam, Cygnific is in charge of the Operational Steering of the B2C units in Enschede with 75FTE, London (Wembley) with 70FTE, Manila with 65FTE and Tirana with 20FTE.

Function description

The Manager Business to Consumer is hierarchically responsible for the department in Amsterdam, but is also responsible for the operational performance of the B2C units in Enschede, Wembley, Manila and Tirana. Together these units contribute to joint KPI’s on e.g. handling times, service levels and quality targets.


The manager B2C is responsible to translate Air France/ KLM and Cygnific customer service strategies into tactical and operational plans and implement and control the appropriate structures, policies and processes in such a way that services will be delivered and that sales will be concluded meeting the agreed targets. The Manager Business to Consumer reports to the Director Operations.

Job requirements

  • University think and work level

  • Work experience in service delivery/travel environment
  • Experience in dealing with a client/supplier relationship
  • Experience with managing large numbers of staff
  • Fluent in Dutch and English, both written and spoken
